Why Visualize? âœ¨

Learning through visualization is a powerful technique because it taps into our brain's natural ability to process visual information far more effectively than text. When you see an algorithm sort an array or find a path, you're not just learning a concept; you're building a mental model. This makes complex, abstract ideas feel concrete and intuitive.

Interactive visualizations enhance memory retention, deepen understanding of efficiency and trade-offs, and make the learning process more engaging and fun. It's the difference between reading a recipe and actually cooking the meal.
